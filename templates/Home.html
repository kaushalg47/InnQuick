How Can We Help?

1. What services do you offer?

We offer room service, concierge assistance, QR code access for check-ins, and detailed room views before booking.

2. How do I check-in?

You can easily check-in using the QR code provided at the hotel entrance or use our app to check-in online.

3. Can I change my booking?

Yes, you can change your booking by visiting the 'My Bookings' section in the app or contacting our support team.

4. What if I need help during my stay?

Our concierge service is available 24/7 to assist you with any requests, from booking tours to arranging transportation.

5. How do I order room service?

You can order room service through the app or by scanning the QR code in your room to view the menu.
